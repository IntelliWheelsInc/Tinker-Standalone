The Tinker System

Tinker is a revolutionary system that allows you to customize any product in a fun and playful way. You can change the colors, learn about your measurements, and order your new product all in one place.

Click Step 1 to learn about the designing process.

or

Step 1: Customize

When designing a customized product, we like to do the fun parts first. The first step is to choose is the color.

Once you've selected the color, hit the next button to get to the next option. Every choice you make updates the live preview in the center of the screen so that you can see what your new wheelchair is going to look like.

With Tinker, you can design your custom product from start to finish in a visual and fun way. Tinker is about choice and giving you the power to build that perfect customized item.

or

Step 2: Measure

Getting your measurements right is the difference between having an OK product, and a GREAT product. That's why the Tinker system gives you videos that explain how to measure yourself for your new chair.

As you progress through the measurements section, you can track your progress with the blue progress bar at the top. At any time, you can save your design with the "Save and Review" button at the bottom right of the screen and come back to it later.


or

Step 3: Order

Once you have the perfect design on the screen, you're ready to order!

The Tinker system can accept orders from anyone, Users, Dealers, or even VA's. Just follow through the prompts on the order page.

or

Step 4: Take a Break! We've got it from here.

A customer service rep will get in touch with you to give you an estimate of how long it will take to build your chair, and we might call you to confirm a few things, but for the most part, your job is done! Now just sit back, and get excited, because your new custom product is on the way.
